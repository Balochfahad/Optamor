Introduction to Dynamic Speaking - for women in work

  • Course Type: Class
  • Duration: 1 Day
  • Price: £180 +VAT
  • Share This Course

Available Dates

There are no available dates for this course.

Aims

It's not what you say, but how you say it

Suitable for those who want to be more dynamic, powerful and memorable, this session is for professionals who want to develop their ability to present & influence with impact and speak with “life & vitality” in challenging business situations. Also very useful for formal/informal presentations in either internal or external environments. Special emphasis is placed on the use of the voice and delivery skills – it’s not just what you say but also how you say it.

Objectives

Maximise planned & spontaneous presentations in the minimum time.

  • Deliver their message with clarity.
  • Effective Vocal Delivery: use their voice powerfully & with authority.
  • Speak with greater confidence and professionalism.
  • Convey a more confident personal presence.

Summary

During the workshop you will:

  • Discover a powerful strategic presentation skills framework.
  • Learn to communicate with “life & vitality” to make a lasting impact.
  • Become more effective and learn to have time-saving discussions & presentations through keys to audience recall & mind-mapping.
  • Learn to speak with clarity and engage your audience by creating a punchy style.
  • Build greater confidence and professionalism.
