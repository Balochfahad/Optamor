Stepping up to Leadership

  • Course Type: Class
  • Duration: 1 Day
  • Price: £250 +VAT
  • Share This Course

Available Dates

There are no available dates for this course.

Aims

Designed to appeal to new and aspiring team leaders. If you are new to the role, you will find this course provides an understanding of the team leadership skills you will need to progress and how to develop them.

Objectives

  • Understand the role, responsibilities and function of the team leader
  • Make decisions with greater confidence
  • Motivate and develop the team to achieve greater potential
  • Improve channels of communication within the team
  • Use the stages of team development to assess the maturity of their team
  • Provide constructive feedback for team members


Our training style combines practical skills with proven techniques, we pride ourselves on our ‘fun but informative’ approach and our consistently strong results, we aim to maximise learning outcomes for all participants every time.

Summary

What does it mean to be a team leader in your profession or in the context of your organisation

  • What are the qualities of a good team leader
  • Explore stages of team formation and team dynamics
  • Recognise the main leadership styles and develop your individual style considering aspects such as planning and monitoring work schedules, using key information to solve business critical issues, how to maintain a safe and healthy work environment
  • Translate the goals of the company into measurable individual and team objectives in order to motivate, manage and ‘lead’ your team effectively
  • Handle difficult situations effectively to ensure consistent and strong performance within the team

Personal Action Plans

  • All participants commit to a plan of action that they will implement on return to the workplace so that they derive maximum benefit from our training events and a tangible return on investment is realised within your business
