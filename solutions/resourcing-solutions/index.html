See Resourcing Solutions Differently

Performance focused, people-led

Would you like to harness your culture and attract the right people?

Our Resourcing Solutions are built upon a partnership with your business and a great understanding of who you are. It is only from here that we present the solutions to help your organisation perform and grow with the right people.

Do you want people who can do the job, and also love what they do?

The right people for your organisation aren’t necessarily the ones with just the right experience and skills; we believe that finding the person who loves what they do and are aligned to your culture is equally vital. Skills can be taught, but behaviour and passion can’t, and it is these passionate people who will help your business grow. Getting the right fit means understanding who you are as a business, defining what you stand for and living your core values. Getting this right in partnership with Optamor will not only define your culture but ensure that it is communicated to both internal an external talent to attract, acquire and retain the right people.

Our solution doesn’t stop at a hire, it starts there!

Once the right people have been hired we guide and support them into your organisation through a thorough on-boarding process. As a people solutions partner we work with you throughout the resourcing journey to make sure that your new hire has the right experience that enables and empowers them to perform in their role.

Seeing things differently

Through our people solutions framework we aim to acquire, evaluate, engage and develop; a process that requires us to partner with you from the very beginning, establishing who you are in order to understand your culture, where you want to be and what skills, beliefs and values you look for when aligning people to your business.
