Unlocking the power of insight

The power of analytics to transform your business

For most organisations people are their largest cost and investment, we therefore use people analytics to help you understand and explore how you harness the power of your people.

People, not data points

We are passionate about people and not seeing them as data points enables us to consider innovation and creativity. Working with you we explore what is having an impact on your people and develop methods and techniques to build an engaged and empowered workforce where their capability is valued and drives performance.

PEOPLE INTELLIGENCE

To determine the right people solutions for you we must understand not only your company but the people within it and those you are trying to attract. What do your workforce and potential candidates think of you, how do they behave and how can we predict their performance? We use our experience and expertise to look into the psychology behind the science and build solutions that work.

ANALYTICS & INSIGHT

You can trust that our insights, suggestions and opinions are based on in-depth analytics and analysis into the marketplace trends that could have an impact on your business. We harness our knowledge and connections with recognised external specialists to provide you with knowledge, guidance and support where and when you need it the most.

DIAGNOSTICS & AUDITS

By using the right tools collated in the right way, we’re able to look and discover key information about you and your position in the market. We can explore new methods, techniques and processes and understand why specific ones haven’t worked for you in the past. Together we can explore our findings and help you produce the results you need.  

OPTAMOR QI

Networking optional, learning essential! What is the true value of knowledge if it is not shared? We have therefore created Optamor QI to be our vessel from which to share knowledge, insights, analytics and help to connect interesting ideas, uncover geniuses and find practical ways of growing a business with our members.

Optamor QI is a customer driven service that allows us to put members at the heart of what we do and our customers perform and grow through their people. Designed around learning and sharing knowledge, we’re providing strong, practical guidance and advice on all things people!
