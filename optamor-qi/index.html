DISCOVER YOUR QI MOMENT

Optamor QI was created to provide our members access to a confidential learning group of like-minded professionals and market leading experts to discuss, debate and explore practical solutions to real business challenges. Optamor QI is our way of opening our doors, sharing our expertise and starting conversations between fellow professionals with real business goals. Created for our customers and our collaborators, we believe that inspired and empowered minds see things differently.

What is the true value of knowledge if it is not shared?

We know just how hard it is for business professionals to find the time to invest in their own development. We try and make it easy for our members to gain access to practical guidance, speak to someone about a real business challenge or catch up on the outputs of one of our learning groups. However we are always amazed the time our members invest if it’s fun, educational and provides real value to them and their business.
