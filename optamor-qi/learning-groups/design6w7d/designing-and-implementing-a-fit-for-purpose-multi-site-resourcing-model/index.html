Designing and Implementing a fit for purpose multi-site Resourcing model

  • Event Type: Class
  • Duration: 3.5 Hours
  • Price: £0 +VAT
  • Share This Event

Available Dates

There are no available dates for this event.

Aims

This Resourcing Think Tank will look to address and understand some of the key strategic issues businesses face when designing and implementing complex multi-site resourcing operating models. It will explore the benefits and complexities of utilising a central resourcing ‘hub’, whilst balancing this against the correct on-site presence to partner the business, to create and evolve the correct behaviours amongst the hiring manager community and ensure candidates gain a fit for purpose experience.

It will also explore best practices around implementing this style of model out across a business that has traditionally delivered resourcing at a local level.

Objectives

Some of the questions we will be looking to answer are:

  • What is the correct balance of on-site vs off-site recruiters?
  • What level of skills and experience are required across the model?
  • What lessons can resourcing learn from the HR Central Service model?

    As the market continues to evolve and becomes evermore competitive to secure the right talent, requiring an uplift in the the skill sets of team members, this promises to be a lively and interesting debate so secure your seat today; tickets will sell out fast.
