Optimum Human Performance

  • Event Type: Class
  • Duration: 2.5 Hours
  • Price: £0 +VAT
  • Share This Event

Available Dates

There are no available dates for this event.

Aims

This learning session will explore the impact nutrition, exercise and sleep have on mental performance. We will share research and introduce practical tips that can have a breath-taking impact on an individual’s ability to work at peak performance.

The session will cover how all four areas of science are co-dependent, and exactly what Optimum Human Performance means for the workplace. It will draw on research-based medical evidence that tells us you simply cannot be at your best without working on all four areas, and that some of the by-products of a healthy programme reach far wider than just personal performance.

Objectives

The first part of the morning will be conducted by CHX founder, Damien Stork. Damien was a professional sportsman turned personal trainer who has spent the past 15 years building a successful private-equity backed business in recruitment and talent management which he exited in 2013. He will be introducing the Optimum Human Performance programme and sharing some key insights on Food, sleep and exercise before leading a Q&A session.

The second part of the morning will welcome Dr. Chris Beedie who will talk about Psychological Skills Training and how you can build on good nutrition, sleep and exercise to take mental function to an altogether new level - addressing confidence, motivation, decision-making and emotional intelligence.

Summary

09:30 – Breakfast and Introductions
10:00 – Learning Session
11:45 – Finish
11:45 – Ask the expert session (Dr Chris Beedie)

Please arrive at 9:30 for breakfast and introductions as the learning session will start promptly at 10:00am. This session is planned to finish at 11:45 and you will then have the opportunity to join our ‘ask the expert’ session that will run directly after the learning group.
