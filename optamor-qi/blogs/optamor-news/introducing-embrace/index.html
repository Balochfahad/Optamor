Serocor Group Introduce Embrace

  • By Mike Gawthorne
  • On 09 March 2015
  • in Corporate Social Responsibility, Charity, Embrace
  • 0 Comments
  • Share:

Through the passions of our people we encourage positive social impact

We have launched 'Embrace', our new corporate social initiative that forms part of the Serocor Group’s ongoing and dedicated commitment to supporting charitable causes. This mechanism includes team fundraising events, volunteering and community programmes that are close to our hearts.

The Embrace initiative is driven and inspired by our employees who are passionate about supporting charities, volunteering and community initiatives. It is also heavily supported by Group CEO, Mike Gawthorne, who is enthusiastic and dedicated to our business helping to shape a better world.  This initiative enables us to raise awareness for our collectively chosen charities allowing us to give something back to our society, as everyone at one point in time can be affected by the causes we support.

We look forward to embracing our ethos to help shape a better world...  and have some fun whilst doing it!
