Paul Mallinson

Managing Director

As Managing Director of Optamor my role is to provide leadership and direction to all staff, partners and associates ensuring we challenge ourselves to pro-actively work with customers helping them see things differently in order to build people solutions that improve their business performance. 

My belief is that we should be a values based company that puts relationships, accountability and integrity ahead of short term commercial gain. 

My experience in leading a team that builds business enhancing people solutions spans the last 30 years and what I have learnt is that at the heart of a successful business relationship there are a group of people that will come together with a shared vision and common goals. They will have respect for each other, trust each other and when times are challenging grow stronger together as a team to improve the service offering and ultimately, business performance. Without shared values and beliefs, business relationships tend to be short lived and this is why at Optamor we build solutions rather than “bid” for them ensuring that those shared values and goals are ever present in our service offerings.

Why do you like working for Optamor?

I like working for Optamor because I am surrounded by like-minded individuals who want to do the very best for their customers, their colleagues and themselves. Our team knows that success lies in being accountable, responsible and doing the things they said they would do.

How do your customers see you?

I believe our customers see us as like-minded individuals that have knowledge and experience in building people solutions that are fit for them now but agile enough to constantly change and evolve to meet the rapidly changing landscape that their businesses face.
