Mike Gawthorne

Group CEO

I work closely with Paul and the team to help devise, support and strategise bespoke solutions for customers, whilst helping to set the strategic direction of Optamor keeping in mind market trends and technologies.

I have worked in the talent management arena since 1997, prior to this Engineering. Over the years I have sold, developed and implemented a number of bespoke solutions for customers across the portfolio of services Optamor provides.

Interesting thing about you that not many people know

Unfortunately my wife and I got caught in the 2004 tsunami in Thailand, it wasn’t a great experience, however it has taught us to appreciate life and particularly the kindness of mankind! That’s a bit deep!

Why do you like working for Optamor?

Optamor and the people who work within the organisation are passionate about finding solutions to help companies grow, I get a real kick out of this, especially when you are working with like-minded individuals

How do your customers see you?

Passionate about finding a solution that meets the customer's need, whilst working in a consultative fashion to define and support the end customer's company objectives.
