Kerry Marchbank

Head of Marketing

I am responsible for developing the market position of Optamor as an expert brand in the Talent Solutions space, as well as driving initiatives that support business development and sales engagement. My role is varied and I love shaping the face of this dynamic business which is constantly evolving, as is the market we operate in.

I graduated from the University of Plymouth as Marketing Student of the Year and have since put 20 years experience under my belt. I have had the pleasure of working both in-house and as a consultant, helping over 20 organisations to take their marketing (and business) to the next level by adopting a strategic approach. I am a strong marketing all-rounder with a background primarily in Technology, Telecommunications and Professional Service sectors, working with start-ups through to market leaders.

Interesting thing about you that not many people know

Although I’d never parted with British soil until the age of 19, travelling has become my passion and sole reason for coming to work. Despite what people say the world is a big place, there is still so much to see and do!

Why do you like working for Optamor?

Primarily it’s the people, not just because we are good to work with, but we are genuine experts and it is great to be surrounded by that level of expertise on a daily basis.

How do your customers see you?

Passionate about what I do, take ownership in delivering what I believe is the best solution to achieve the required outcome and having the courage to challenge when needed.
